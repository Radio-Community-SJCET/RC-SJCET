About Us

Radio Community SJCET, a student-run community that has been amplifying student talent and the vibrant voice of SJCET since October 2021.Starting with a spark of enthusiasm, we've empowered students to tune into innovation and collaboration from a small group of fellow enthsiasts to a thriving team of students. As a dynamic and passionate group of students from St.Joseph's College of Engineering and Technology Palai, we're dedicated to exploring the world of radio communication and connect with like-minded enthusiasts. We mainly concentrate on the Amateur radio operation, license support, emergency communication, related exhibitions, events and so on. Join the movement where creativity meets passion, and where every voice matters.
